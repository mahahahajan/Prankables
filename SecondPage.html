SHAMPOO EXPLODER

July 7, 2011 October 21, 2014 Theme Buster

Want to waste your friends shampoo? Prank them in the shower? Get revenge for something they did to you? Need an April Fool's Prank?

This is the prank for you. All you need for this prank is a shampoo bottle (opaque), vinegar, baking soda, and a thin napkin, like a tissue or toilet paper. With a little time and patience, you can get your friends with a prank that they will never forget!

Step 1

July 2, 2014 October 20, 2014 Theme Buster panache 1 Comment

+ You will need a bottle that's opaque (not see through) so that your victim cannot see the slightly different coloured goo in the bottle.

+ Also, it needs to be empty.

+ Once you've picked your bottle, rinse out the bottle, being careful not to wash off the dried shampoo from underneath the cap.

+ Making the bottle too clean will give the game away!
(If bottle is new, don't worry about it);

Step 2

July 2, 2014 October 20, 2014 Theme Buster panache 1 Comment

+ Fill the shampoo bottle 1/2 - 3/4 the way up with vinegar.

+ Make sure there is still room for your full sized napkin/tissue to fit without touching the vinegar.

+ Set the bottle aside.

Step 2

July 2, 2014 October 20, 2014 Theme Buster panache 1 Comment

+ Pour some baking powder onto the napkin.

+ Slowly roll the napkin up, being careful not to let any baking soda spill.

+ Roll the napkin so that the baking soda will not directly touch the vinegar at first.

Step 3

July 2, 2014 October 20, 2014 Theme Buster panache 1 Comment

+ Finally, uncap the vinegar filled shampoo bottle.

+ Slowly lower the baking soda napkin in and quickly close the cap.

+ Very carefully set it down in the shower, and walk away.

Step 4

July 2, 2014 October 20, 2014 Theme Buster panache 1 Comment

+ Stay within ear shot

+ Enjoy the sufferings of your friends!
